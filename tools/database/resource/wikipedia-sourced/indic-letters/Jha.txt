==Comparison of Jha==
The various Indic scripts are generally related to each other through adaptation and borrowing, and as such the glyphs for cognate letters, including Jha, are related as well.  Exceptionally, the glyph of the Lao character used for Jha is a modification of [[Ja (Indic)|Ja]].
